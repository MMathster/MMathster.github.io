### Independent Mathematical Research

*2017 – Present*

My independent work began with geometric problem construction and has evolved
into formal analytic investigation. The through-line is consistent: identifying
non-obvious structure in classical configurations and extending it into
generalized domains.

**Current focus — Higher Analysis**

Structural analysis of special functions and integral classes, including
generalized hypergeometric functions \\({}_{p}F_{q}\\), polylogarithmic
integrals, and integrals involving floor and fractional-part functions.
Work is produced in LaTeX and verified computationally using Python and
Mathematica.

**Geometry — Loci of Kimberling Triangle Centers**

Derivation of parametric loci for selected centers from Clark Kimberling's
*Encyclopedia of Triangle Centers* under the Thales configuration: right
triangles inscribed in a fixed circle. Results range from standard conics
to quartic curves with geometric singularities.

**Problem construction and exposition**

Original problem sets and solution writing spanning secondary through advanced
undergraduate level, including contributions produced in English, Spanish,
Japanese, and Traditional Chinese.
